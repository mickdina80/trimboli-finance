Credit Guide

Credit Representative No. 478816

This Credit Guide is provided to you in accordance with the National Consumer Credit Protection Act 2009 (Cth) (NCCP Act). It contains important information about who we are, the services we provide, how we are paid, our responsible lending obligations, and your rights and our dispute resolution process.

1. Who We Are

Trimboli Finance ("Trimboli Finance", "we", "us", "our") provides mortgage broking and credit assistance services to consumers in Australia. Trimboli Finance is authorised as a Credit Representative (No. 478816).

2. Our Credit Services

We provide credit assistance services including:

  • Assessing your financial situation
  • Identifying suitable loan products
  • Recommending appropriate lenders
  • Assisting with loan applications
  • Submitting loan documentation to lenders

We do not provide financial product advice unless separately authorised.

3. Our Lender Panel

We source loans from a panel of lenders. A list of lenders is available upon request. If we are unable to assist you with your loan requirements, we will inform you.

4. Responsible Lending Obligations

Under the NCCP Act, we must:

  • Make reasonable inquiries about your requirements and objectives
  • Make reasonable inquiries about your financial situation
  • Take reasonable steps to verify your financial information
  • Assess whether a proposed credit contract is "not unsuitable" for you

If we recommend a loan, we must have a reasonable basis for that recommendation. You may request a written copy of our assessment within 7 years of the credit assistance being provided.

5. How We Are Paid

Upfront Commission

Generally between 0% and 0.85% of the loan amount (excl. GST), paid by the lender upon settlement.

Trail Commission

Generally between 0% and 0.40% per annum of the outstanding loan balance, paid while your loan remains active.

Consumer Fees

In most cases you will not pay us a fee. If a fee is payable, we will disclose it in writing before proceeding.

6. Volume-Based Incentives

We may receive additional benefits from lenders based on overall loan volumes, campaign-based incentives, or aggregator arrangements. These arrangements do not impact our obligation to recommend loans that are not unsuitable for you.

7. Ownership and Associations

Trimboli Finance is not owned by, nor does it own, any lender. We do not have a conflict of interest that affects our credit assistance.

We maintain Professional Indemnity Insurance that complies with the NCCP Act. This insurance covers claims relating to credit services we provide.
